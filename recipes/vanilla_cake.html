Vanilla Cake Recipe

Description

This vanilla cake is the result of bringing together the best of Japanese sponge cakes and Western butter based cakes.

It has the world renowned very soft, fluffy crumb of Japanese cakes and uses the Japanese baking method, combined with the buttery goodness and sweetness of Western cakes.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Vanilla extract
  • Sugar
  • Time

How To Make It

  1. Mix the eggs with the sugar
  2. Add the milk, the oil, and the vanilla
