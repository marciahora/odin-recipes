Oreo cookies recipe

Description

Who doesn't love Oreos? I know I do.

This recipe will have a special place in your heart. It's easy to make and AMAZINGLY good.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Oreo Cookies

How To Make It

  1. Mix the eggs and the sugar
  2. Add the butter to the mix
  3. Add the Oreo Cookies
  4. Add the Flour
  5. Make little balls with the dough and bake for 10 min.
