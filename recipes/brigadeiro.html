Brigadeiro - The brazilian recipe

What is this delicious thing?

So, brigadeiro is a recipe from Brazil.

It's made with condensed milk, chocolate and butter.

And I have to say, it's AMAZING. You must try!

Ingredients

  • 395g of Condensed milk
  • 2 Ts of Powdered Chocolate
  • 1 Ts of Butter

How to make it

  1. Mix the Condensed milk with the Powdered Chocolate in a pan
  2. Once everything is well mixed, turn on the oven and start to mix it.
  3. Don't stop mixing until the brigadeiro starts to come off the pan
  4. Add the butter and mix to the chocolate
  5. Okay, now it's the important part. ENJOY IT!
